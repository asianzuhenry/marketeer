# Marketeer
Marketeer is an online platform that connects buyers and sellers of marketing services. It provides a marketplace for businesses to find marketing professionals and agencies to help them grow their brand.

## Features
- **User Profiles**: Create and manage profiles for buyers and sellers.
- **Service Listings**: Sellers can list their marketing services with detailed descriptions and pricing.
- **Search and Filter**: Buyers can search for services based on various criteria such as location, price, and service type.
- **Messaging System**: Built-in messaging system for buyers and sellers to communicate directly.
- **Reviews and Ratings**: Buyers can leave reviews and ratings for sellers based on their experience.
- **Secure Payments**: Integrated payment system to facilitate secure transactions between buyers and sellers.
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Sellers can access analytics to track their service performance and customer engagement.
